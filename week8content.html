Lab Tasks

Tasks 1 & 2

For this task I cropped my audio of newsroom chatter to a more suitable portion. After this I exported derivative audio files in different siund qualities and formats, each of which I experimented editing with. I have attached all of these files below.

  1. WAV file 001

    • Bass and treble, distortion and wahwah added to file.
    • The 'wahwah' effect is very over-powering, you cannot hear much of the original file. It sort of sounds like a news signal being lost, etc.

  2. MP3 medium stereo 002

    • Fade in: audio gradually gets louder.
    • Applied high-pass filter (1000 Hz, 48dB): removes low frequency sounds. The background noises within the audio, like camera clicks, etc ae much louder than voices.

  3. MP3 standard stereo 003

    • Echo (delay time 1 second & decay: 1)
    • The audio gradually gets nosier and a lot busier as all the echoes merge together.
    • I find this reflective of how noisy our heads can get with the amount of news sources that are out there; this is an auditorial representation of the anxiety an overhwelming amount of information causes as the noise gets louder and louder with every news source competing for attention.

  4. MP3 medium mono 004

    • Applied filter curve EQ. Heightened it so the audio seems extra loud.
